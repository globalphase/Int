About Us

Who we are and why we do what we do

Our Story

GlobalPhase International was founded to meet the growing demand for efficient and transparent international trade solutions. With years of industry experience and a vast global network, we assist businesses in navigating the complex landscape of cross-border commerce. As a fully online operation, we provide agile, cost-effective services tailored to the unique needs of our clients.

We believe that every business, regardless of size, should have access to reliable import-export support. Our team is dedicated to helping our clients succeed in the global market by offering sourcing assistance, logistics coordination, customs support, documentation, and strategic consultation. At GlobalPhase International, we don’t just move goods — we build lasting trade partnerships.

Our Values

  • Customer-Centric: Our clients are at the heart of everything we do. We go the extra mile to meet their needs.
  • Innovation: We continuously adapt and innovate to stay ahead in the ever-changing global trade landscape.
